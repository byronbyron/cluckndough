Cluck n Dough is a husband and wife street food operation with a shared love for clucking great food. Expect to find delicious flame grilled chicken burgers, Asian style, along with influences from around the world. Not forgetting the mouth-watering loaded fries with a unique twist and authentic Neapolitan sourdough pizza. Our menus rotate from cluck (the chicken) to dough (the pizza) across a variety of events throughout the year.

We are also available for private catering. Interested? We would love to hear from you!
